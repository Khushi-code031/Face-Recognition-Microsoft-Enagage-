What is a Smart Lock and What is Atoms?

A Smart Lock is a simple device which unlocks and locks the door by means of a virtual key. This key can be your fingerprint, voice or the iris of your eye. Atoms introduces a newer, and a better approch by recognizing your Face and immediately opens the door.
